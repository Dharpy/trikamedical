About Us

TriKa Medical and Medicine Supply Corporation is a company which aims to provide Complete Medical Solution for every part of the Healthcare Facility in Medical Supplies, Medicine, Biomedical Services and Medical Engineering Product. ESTABLISHED SINCE 2017, the company continuously offers essential, caliber and novel medical engineering products, equipment and medicines from distinguished and established companies and manufacturers. Our purpose includes making these medical products and technical services to become more obtainable and available to the country’s expanding healthcare market and needs.

TriKa Medical offers a wide range of medical engineering products and medical equipment; offering a high quality of engineering services from the period of design, project implementation, installation and testing/commissioning. Beyond the Company’s core competence is the services and after sales support program to our supplied equipment, always striving forward with enthusiasm and sense of urgency.

The company is composed and represented by highly knowledgeable and trained biomedical engineers, project engineers and other healthcare professionals whom will cater and assist the needs of the customers and partners.
